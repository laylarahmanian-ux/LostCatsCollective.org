How It Works

Three simple steps from art to impact

1

Scan & Donate

See a Lost Cats sign in your neighborhood. Scan the QR code. Donate securely in under 60 seconds with Apple Pay, Google Pay, or credit card.

2

We Buy Gift Cards

100% of your donation becomes gift cards for groceries, gas, and essentials. Every $25 equals one gift card. No overhead, no middlemen.

3

Direct Distribution

Gift cards are distributed with dignity to neighbors experiencing homelessness. People choose what they need, when they need it.

Why Gift Cards?

A dignified approach to real help

Dignity

People choose what they need—groceries, gas, hygiene products. Not what we think they need.

Safety

No cash handling. Secure digital donations. Traceable, transparent impact.

Practical

Kroger, Meijer, Speedway, CVS—stores people already use. Real needs, real solutions.

Direct

No middlemen. No overhead in our pilot phase. Your $25 becomes a $25 gift card. Simple.

The Art

Local artists creating signs that start conversations

Each Lost Cats sign is a unique piece of art created by local artists. The "lost cat" aesthetic is universal—everyone stops, everyone looks. But these signs point to a different kind of lost: our neighbors experiencing homelessness.

Art has the power to make us pause, to make us feel, to make us act. That's what Lost Cats Collective is about—using creativity to channel empathy into tangible help.
